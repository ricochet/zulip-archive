Topic: WASI support for Kotlin

Sébastien Deleuze (Jan 14 2023 at 17:25):

FYI I am starting working actively on WASI and Wasm Component Model support for Kotlin, you can follow this work on https://github.com/sdeleuze/kowasm. I may submit a related PR to https://github.com/bytecodealliance/wit-bindgen later. In case you want to share the news, there are Twitter and Mastodon related announcements.

Today, I am pleased to announce the creation of KoWasm, an experimental project that will provide WASI and Wasm components support for Kotlin/Wasm. https://github.com/sdeleuze/kowasm Let's explore how far we can go with full stack development using @kotlin and #WebAssembly!
