Brice Dobry (Apr 09 2021 at 20:13):

Hi all. I saw some work on targeting RISC-V and wanted to let the community know about the newly launched Managed Runtimes SIG from RISC-V International. Our goals will be to coordinate various groups working on managed runtimes on RISC-V and help to identify areas for collaboration, evaluate hardware extensions, etc. You can find the group's charter here - https://github.com/riscv/groups/blob/main/Managed-Runtimes/CHARTER.md. If you are interested, please join our mailing list for the latest updates (https://lists.riscv.org/g/sig-managed-runtimes). We plan to announce our first meeting later this month, after we gather an audience.
