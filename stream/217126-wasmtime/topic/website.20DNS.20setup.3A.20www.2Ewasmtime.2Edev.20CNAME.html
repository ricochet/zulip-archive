Topic: website DNS setup: www.wasmtime.dev CNAME

Chris Fallin (Aug 18 2023 at 02:30):

A random realization while attempting to visit our website today: the usual www subdomain (www.wasmtime.dev) doesn't exist, only the apex (wasmtime.dev). This is a little surprising and at least old-internet fingers like mine sometimes type it subconsciously...

One common setup (AFAIK) is to have www. be a CNAME (DNS alias record) to the apex domain (in BIND syntax, www.wasmtime.dev. IN CNAME wasmtime.dev.). We seem to have this right for cranelift.dev (www.cranelift.dev is an alias for cranelift.dev), so it's a quirk of wasmtime.dev's setup.

Not a huge huge deal but worth noting -- cc @David Bryant or @Till Schneidereit perhaps?
