Till Schneidereit (Aug 07 2023 at 20:26):

I also don't see an agenda, does everyone spend the day at the event?

The agenda will be more formally published soon, but in the meantime, here's a rough outline:

  • we'll start the day with a short opening, followed by an overview of the key toolchains as well as some suggestions for projects to tackle, etc
  • then the majority of the day will be spent with people working on using components, compiling code to components, etc. For those attendees who don't yet have significant experience with components, we'll make this a very hands-on experience, with lots of support and explanation of things, so everyone should come away from this with a much deeper understanding of the component model
  • towards the end of this time, we'll ask attendees to share their results, key learnings, and problems they ran into
  • between 4pm and 5pm, we'll wrap up the day by giving an overview of the results of that last point
  • and finally, we'll invite everyone to join us for a happy hour/after party to close out the day/week
