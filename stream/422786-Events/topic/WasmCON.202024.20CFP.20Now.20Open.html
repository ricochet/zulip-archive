Liam Randall (May 03 2024 at 17:00):

The LinuxFoundation's decision to host Kubecon in Utah was made years in advance of Utah's HB257 and given the scale of the event, is not easy to undwind. This bill was passed after Kubecon was committed to SLC, but obviously we moved the WasmCon to co-locate with Kubecon this Fall. I believe that the LF, including both the CNCF, Kubecon, and wasmDay hold a very high bar for DEI, and as a regular co-chair have worked hard to meet those standards.

As someone who does care about this, legislative bills like this is very hard to plan around. According to the CNCF blog, "In 2023 alone, over 520 anti-LGBTQ+ bills were introduced in U.S. state legislatures. The ACLU is currently tracking 387 anti-LGBTQ bills across 37 U.S. states." It is a sad situation.
